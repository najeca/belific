Privacy Policy

Belific — Effective date: 8 June 2026

1. Overview

Belific is a personal scheduling app for iPhone. It helps you plan your day, track events, and set local reminders. This policy explains what data the app stores, and what it does not collect.

2. Data We Store

Belific stores the following data locally on your device only, using iOS AsyncStorage:

  • Events you create — title, category, times, notes, and date
  • Timer settings (focus and break durations)
  • Notification preferences (enabled or disabled)
  • A first-launch flag to manage the onboarding state

None of this data ever leaves your device. There is no account, no sign-in, and no server that receives your information.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

Belific does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your IP address or device identifiers
  • Your location
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data via third-party SDKs or services

There are no analytics tools, no advertising networks, and no third-party data sharing of any kind built into Belific.

4. Notifications

Belific schedules notifications entirely on your device using iOS's local notification system. No notification data is sent to any external server. If you disable notifications, no data is affected — only the on-device reminders are cancelled.

5. Children

Belific is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted to this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
